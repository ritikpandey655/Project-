Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 21, 2025

PYQverse ("we", "our", or "us") operates the PYQverse mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you:

  • Create an Account: Name, email address, and profile picture (from Google Sign-In)
  • Use the Service: Practice history, exam preferences, bookmarked questions
  • Subscribe to Pro: Payment information (processed securely by PhonePe/Razorpay)
  • Contact Support: Your email and message content

1.2 Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you use our Service:

  • Device information (device type, operating system, browser type)
  • Usage patterns (pages visited, time spent, features used)
  • Performance data (crash reports, error logs)
  • Analytics data (via Firebase Analytics)

1.3 AI-Generated Content

When you use our AI features:

  • Questions you generate are processed by Google Gemini or Groq AI
  • Images you upload for doubt solving are analyzed by AI
  • This data is processed in real-time and not stored permanently

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Service Provision: To provide and maintain our Service
  • Personalization: To customize your experience based on exam preferences
  • Performance Tracking: To track your progress, streaks, and analytics
  • AI Features: To generate questions and solve doubts using AI
  • Communication: To send important updates, notifications, and promotional content
  • Improvement: To analyze usage and improve our Service
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security issues
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Firebase Firestore: For user profiles, practice history, and bookmarks
  • Firebase Authentication: For secure Google Sign-In
  • Encryption: All data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS/SSL
  • One-Device Login: Session management to prevent unauthorized access

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, analytics
  • Google Gemini AI: AI-powered question generation and doubt solving
  • Groq AI: Alternative AI provider for question generation
  • PhonePe/Razorpay: Payment processing for Pro subscriptions
  • Vercel: Web hosting and CDN

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services that help us operate (Firebase, AI providers, payment processors)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business Transfer: In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale
  • Safety: To protect the rights, safety, or property of PYQverse, users, or the public

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update incorrect or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from promotional emails
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@pyqverse.in

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Understand how you use our Service
  • Improve user experience
  • Analyze service performance

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect Service functionality.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as:

  • Your account is active
  • Needed to provide the Service
  • Required for legal obligations
  • Necessary to resolve disputes

After account deletion, we may retain some data for legal compliance or legitimate business purposes.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than India, including the United States (Firebase, Google, Vercel). We ensure adequate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an email notification (for significant changes)

Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. GDPR Compliance (For EU Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent

13. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect
  • Know whether we sell or disclose your personal information
  • Say no to the sale of personal information
  • Access your personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights
